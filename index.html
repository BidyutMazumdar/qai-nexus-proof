SAIIS (VGX)

SAIIS (Sovereign AI Infrastructure Intelligence System) is a research initiative focused on artificial intelligence governance, infrastructure intelligence, benchmarking methodologies, digital sovereignty, computational governance, reproducible analytics, and long-term research preservation.

The initiative supports analytical research, governance evaluation, infrastructure assessment, methodological transparency, reproducibility, and archival preservation through DOI-linked scholarly resources and structured research dissemination.
