
### PCA Results

Principal Component Analysis was applied to reduce
dimensionality and identify latent structures.

The extracted components explained approximately
6.0% of total variance.

(See Figure 01)


### TOPSIS Results

The composite index was calculated using TOPSIS.

The resulting ranking framework enables
cross-country comparison of capability performance.

(See Figure 02–03)


### XGBoost Results

Feature importance analysis identified
hdi
as one of the strongest predictors
within the model.

(See Figure 04)


### Monte Carlo Robustness

Monte Carlo simulation was conducted
to evaluate stability under stochastic perturbation.

The results indicate robustness of the framework
under repeated randomized trials.

(See Figure 05)


### Bootstrap Validation

Bootstrap resampling was employed
to assess statistical stability.

Results support consistency
of the estimated relationships.

(See Figure 06)


### Sensitivity Analysis

Sensitivity analysis assessed
the influence of perturbations
on composite rankings.

The framework demonstrated
acceptable resilience.

(See Figure 07)
